About Our College

Our Legacy

Ram Krishna Paramhans Inter College, located in the heart of Nagal, Saharanpur, stands as a beacon of quality education in the region. Established with a vision to provide comprehensive education from secondary to intermediate levels, our institution has been nurturing young minds for over two decades.

Our Mission

To create an environment where students can achieve academic excellence while developing strong moral character, leadership qualities, and a sense of social responsibility. We believe in holistic development that encompasses intellectual, physical, and spiritual growth.
